Frequently asked questions

What do I need to open a Barclays Bank Account?

If you’re opening an account through your app, you’ll need proof of identity, three years of address history and a UK mobile number.

We accept these documents as proof of identity:

  • Passport
  • UK, EU or EEA full driving licence.

You can download the Barclays app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

If you don’t have a UK mobile number, or one of the documents listed above, you can apply for a Barclays Bank Account online.

If you need any help or to talk about alternative ways to open an account, please call us on 0345 7345 345. We’re here Monday to Friday from 8am to 8pm, and Saturday from 9am to 5pm. We may monitor and record phone calls. Call charges.

Can I get an overdraft with my current account?

Yes, we offer arranged overdrafts with our current accounts. An arranged overdraft allows you to borrow money through your current account. All overdrafts are subject to application, financial circumstances and borrowing history.

How long does it take to open a Barclays Bank Account through the Barclays app?

Applying for a Barclays Bank Account on the app should take 10 to 15 minutes. If your application is approved, then you’ll get your account details right away and can start using the account immediately. Your Barclays debit card will be sent separately – it’ll arrive within seven working days. You PIN will be available within the app in 24 hours.

I've just moved to the country – can I open a Barclays Bank Account?

Yes, anyone with a passport is eligible to open a Barclays Bank Account through the Barclays app. If you are looking to use an eVisa then an appointment will need to be made in Branch. You’ll need proof of your identity (ID) and your UK address. Book an appointment
