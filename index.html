Ways to Bank

Online Banking

Join over 13 million people who bank with us digitally

Prefer to do your banking on a bigger screen? We’ve been improving Online Banking(1) to make it even easier to use.

If you're a victim of fraud or a scam, you're covered by our Online Banking guarantee

Manage your money with Online Banking

Manage money securely

See your balance, transfer between accounts and search transactions.

Control your payments

Make UK and international payments, schedule standing orders and stop a cheque.

Paperwork in one place

See statements, request paper copies, and download financial records.

Let Online Banking save you a trip or a call

You'd be surprised at the things you can do in Online Banking – many things you might have thought you'd need to speak to us about can all be done from your browser.

Set alerts

Keep on top of your finances and protect yourself from fraud by setting alerts to get balance updates as well as payment and overdraft notifications.

Update your details

Keeping your details up to date helps prevent fraud. Change your address, contact number and other personal details online in just a few steps.

Access Smart Investor

If you're thinking of investing, Smart Investor gives you the choice of thousands of investments to help you achieve your financial goals.

Open a savings account

See your savings options and apply for a savings account. You can also track and manage your savings, so you're always in control.

Apply for a loan

Get your loan rate before you apply without affecting your credit score. Loans are subject to application and financial circumstances.

Manage your card

You can check your PIN, report a lost or stolen card and order a new one – you can even track your new card's journey to you.

Join Premier Banking

Along with our signature black debit card, you'll enjoy exclusive Premier rewards and benefits. Requirements and terms apply.

Get insurance

See your options for life insurance, travel and breakdown insurance or mobile phone and gadget insurance – all in Online Banking.

Need help logging in?

Get help with the most common error codes you see in Online Banking. Our tool will tell you what your error code means and what to do next.

Other ways to bank with us

There are many ways to bank with us, whether you'd like to speak to us face-to-face, give us a call or manage your money from your mobile.

Keep your money safe

Fraud and scam prevention

Anyone can be tricked by a scammer – they change tactics often. Stay one step ahead of criminals by keeping yourself updated on the latest scams and following best practices for online security.

Added security with PINSentry

PINSentry is a tool that generates unique eight-digit codes you can use to confirm your identity, authorise transactions and log in to Online Banking – giving you full access to all the features.
